Training Guidelines & Best Practices

Photo Quality Guidelines

  • Multiple Angles: Front-facing, slight left/right turns
  • Lighting Variations: Natural light, indoor lighting, different times
  • Expression Variety: Neutral, smiling, different expressions
  • Distance Variations: Close-up, medium distance shots
  • Clear Images: High resolution, not blurry
  • Minimal Obstructions: No sunglasses, hats if possible

Training Optimization

  • Minimum Photos: 3 photos (5-10 recommended)
  • Quality over Quantity: Better few good photos than many poor ones
  • Regular Updates: Retrain periodically with new photos
  • Test Recognition: Use live camera to verify training results
  • Monitor Accuracy: Check confidence scores during recognition
  • Remove Poor Performers: Delete and retrain low-accuracy faces
